One app, three experiences

Built for customers, stores, and agents.

Waloo adapts to your role while keeping the same clean interface and simple workflow.

Customer

Discover, reserve, pick up.

Browse nearby surplus offers on a map. Adjust your discovery radius. Filter by store type. Reserve with one tap, then pick up using your unique OTP code during the window.

  • Map-based discovery with 1–15 km radius
  • Category filters (bakery, café, grocery…)
  • Credit balance, top-up via card or agent
  • Loyalty points and referral bonuses
  • Push notifications for new offers and pickup reminders
  • Favorites to track preferred stores
Store

List surplus, manage pickups.

Create offers with pricing, pickup windows, and stock counts. Confirm customer pickups by scanning or entering OTP codes. View ratings, manage operating hours, and track revenue.

  • Create and manage item listings with images
  • Set pickup windows and stock quantities
  • Verify pickups via OTP scan or manual entry
  • View customer ratings and reviews
  • Track earnings and credit balance
  • KYC verification for trust and compliance
Agent

Enable cash-in across Algeria.

Agents provide in-person credit top-up services for customers and stores who prefer cash. They keep the ecosystem accessible in areas where digital payments aren't common.

  • Process cash-in requests for customers and stores
  • Set operating hours and availability
  • Track daily activity and commission
  • KYC-verified for security and accountability
  • Visible on the map for nearby users
