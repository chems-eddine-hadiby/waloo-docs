FAQ

Common questions, quick answers.

Is Waloo free to use?

Yes, downloading and using Waloo is completely free. You only pay when you reserve an item using credits.

How do I top up my balance?

You can top up via CIB or Edahabia bank card through Chargily, or in cash at any nearby Waloo agent.

Which cities is Waloo available in?

Waloo works wherever registered stores are active. Offers appear based on your location across Algeria.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your data is encrypted and we never sell it to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What if I miss my pickup window?

The booking is auto-cancelled and credits are returned. Repeated no-shows may incur a small penalty.

How do I become a store or agent?

Sign up in the app, select the "Store" or "Agent" role, then complete KYC verification with your Trade Register and ID.
