Core features

Everything you need to save locally.

Waloo is built around a simple loop: discover nearby deals, reserve with credits, and pick up with a verified code.

01

Map discovery

See stores and offers plotted on a map within your chosen radius (1–15 km). Filter by category: bakery, café, grocery, and more.

02

Instant reservations

One-tap booking locks the item. Credits go into escrow and are released on pickup confirmation or auto-cancelled if expired.

03

OTP pickup

Each reservation generates a unique code. The store scans or enters it to confirm collection — no confusion, no disputes.

04

Credit system

Top up via CIB/Edahabia bank cards through Chargily, or visit a local Waloo agent for cash-in. Credits are in DZD.

05

Loyalty rewards

Earn 1 point per 10 DZD spent. Reach milestones at 100, 250, 500, and 1000 points to unlock credit bonuses.

06

Pickup windows

Every offer has a clear start and end time. A countdown shows exactly how long you have to collect.

07

Push notifications

Get alerted when new offers appear nearby, when your pickup window is closing soon, or when your referral bonus lands.

08

Referral program

Invite friends via your referral link. Both of you earn bonus credits once they make their first booking.
