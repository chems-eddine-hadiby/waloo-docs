Why Waloo exists

Less waste, more value for everyone.

Every day, bakeries, cafés, and grocers across Algeria throw away unsold food. Waloo gives that surplus a second chance — and customers a great deal.

The problem

Local stores lose revenue on surplus stock every single day. Customers nearby would happily buy those items at a discount, but they don't know they exist.

The idea

A map-first mobile app that shows what's available nearby, lets you reserve instantly, and makes pickup simple with a time window and verification code.

The approach

Credit-based payments that work for everyone: top up via bank card online or through a local agent for cash. No barriers, no bank account required.

The result

Stores move their surplus, customers save up to 50%, agents earn commission, and less food ends up in the bin. A win for the whole neighborhood.

🍞

For the community

Every booking means less waste and more affordable food for families who need it.

For local business
